Bruschetta

Bruschetta is an antipasto from Italy consisting of grilled bread rubbed with garlic and topped with olive oil and salt. Variations may include toppings of tomato, vegetables, beans, cured meat, or cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of Italian bread
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Slice the bread into 1/2 inch slices.
  3. Place the bread slices on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake the bread in the oven for 5 minutes, then turn them over and bake for another 5 minutes.
  5. Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool.
  6. Peel the garlic cloves and cut them in half.
  7. Rub the cut side of the garlic over the top of each slice of bread.
  8. Drizzle the olive oil over the bread.
  9. Sprinkle the salt over the top.
  10. Serve and enjoy!

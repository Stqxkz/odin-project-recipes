Cheese Spread

Cheese spread is a spread made from cheese, typically cheddar cheese, that is mixed with other ingredients to make it spreadable. It is often used as a dip for crackers or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup of cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon of hot sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of pepper

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cheddar cheese, mayonnaise, cream cheese, garlic powder, onion powder, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, salt, and pepper.
  2. Mix until well combined.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
  4. Serve with crackers or vegetables.
  5. Enjoy!

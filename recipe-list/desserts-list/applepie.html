Apple Pie

This is a classic apple pie recipe that is perfect for any holiday.

Ingredients

  • 8 small Granny Smith apples, or as needed
  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup packed brown sugar
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 (9 inch) double-crust pie pastry, thawed

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. Peel, core, and slice apples.
  3. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  4. Stir in flour to form a paste.
  5. Add white sugar, brown sugar, and water; bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce temperature and let simmer.
  7. Place the bottom crust in your pan.
  8. Fill with apples, mounded slightly.
  9. Cover with a lattice work crust.
  10. Brush with egg wash and sprinkle with sugar.
  11. Bake 15 minutes in the preheated oven.
  12. Reduce the temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  13. Continue baking for 35 to 45 minutes, until apples are soft.

Appetizers

Here are some appetizer recipes:

  • Guacamole

    • Guacamole is a popular Mexican avocado salad or dip that's quite easy to make. The only trick is to use ripe avocados.

  • Hummus

    • Hummus is a Levantine dip or spread made from cooked, mashed chickpeas or other beans, blended with tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and garlic.

  • Bruschetta

    • Bruschetta is an antipasto from Italy consisting of grilled bread rubbed with garlic and topped with olive oil and salt. Variations may include toppings of tomato, vegetables, beans, cured meat, or cheese.

  • Egg Rolls

    • Spring rolls are a large variety of filled, rolled appetizers or dim sum found in East Asian, South Asian, Middle Eastern, and Southeast Asian cuisine.

  • Cheese Spread

    • Cheese spread is a soft spreadable cheese or processed cheese food product. Various additional ingredients are sometimes used, such as multiple cheeses, fruits, vegetables, herbs, or spices.
